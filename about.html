About All American Tires and Wheels

All American Tires and Wheels serves the new tire and tire service needs of customers in Medford, Ashland, and Grants Pass, OR, as well as surrounding areas.

The History of All American Tires and Wheels

All American Tires and Wheels is located at 940 North Central Ave. Suite B in Medford, OR as a tire dealer and tire service center. We have spent the last 14 years delivering quality tires and services to satisfied customers. Come see us in person, or schedule an appointment online today from the comfort of your couch. Founded in 2001, All American Tires and Wheels is owned by Riaz Ahmad.

At All American Tires and Wheels, you can expect:
  • New and used tires

  • Competitive prices on tire brands like Cooper, Mastercraft, and Goodyear
  • Quality tire services including computer spin balancing, flat tire repairs, low-profile tire installations, PAX tires, tire rotations, and TPMS service and adjustments
  • Auto repair and maintenance services including brake repairs, lube and oil changes, steering and suspension system repairs, and wheel alignments
  • Free brake and alignment checks
  • Free rotation with purchase of four new tire
  • Wheels

Our business is committed to providing high-quality customer service to every person who walks through the door. Our facility is equipped with the latest in tire servicing equipment for an unprecedented level of service.
